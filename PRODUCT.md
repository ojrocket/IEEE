# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Students, current and prospective members, recruiters, and sponsors looking to discover technical chapters, view upcoming events (like hackathons and workshops), read blog posts, and join the chapter.

## Product Purpose
To serve as an immersive, engaging portal for the IEEE Student Branch at SRM University AP, driving member signups and event registrations by showcasing the chapter's technical leadership, workshops, and achievements.

## Brand Personality
Technical, futuristic, and editorial. Evokes visual excitement and forward-looking expertise through a dark theme, neon gold and cyan accent colors, bold display typography, and smooth GSAP/Framer Motion scroll animations.

## Anti-references
- Generic, dry academic portals or institutional lists.
- Bland corporate white pages that lack technical energy.
- Static, low-fidelity landing pages with no movement or interaction.

## Design Principles
- **Technical Editorial Layouts:** Leverage strong contrast, bold display titles, and dynamic layouts rather than repeating identical cards.
- **Immersive Micro-interactions:** Build a sense of high craft with parallax, 3D tilt effects, hover responses, and smooth page transitions.
- **Clear Activity Paths:** Ensure visitors can quickly discover events, learn about individual chapters, and transition smoothly to registration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Maintain high contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1) for all body copy and text tags against dark backdrops.
- Fully respect system-level animation preferences (`prefers-reduced-motion`).
- Ensure navigation and controls are fully accessible via keyboard.
